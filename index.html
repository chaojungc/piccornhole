📖 League Rules

🎯 Match Format

Each match is a best-of-three series played every Wednesday at noon. Two games are played each week (one team gets a bye).

Matches may be rescheduled due to holidays or weather.

⭐ Scoring System

ResultPoints
2 wins (sweep)3 pts
1 win1 pt
0 wins0 pts
No show0 pts

👥 Participation

  • Each team must field at least 2 players per match.
  • Every player must play in at least 2 matches per season.
  • Teams have 7 members each (5 teams, 35 total players).

💰 Entry & Prize

300 NTD per person per season. The entire prize pool goes to the champion team!

Total prize pool: 10,500 NTD

🔄 Draft Process

  • A Throwing Combine (skill showcase) lets captains scout players.
  • Captains compete to determine draft order.
  • Snake draft: 1→2→3→4→5→5→4→3→2→1

📊 Season Format

  • 20-week regular season (Mar 4 – Jul 15)
  • Each team plays 16 games total
  • Top 4 teams advance to playoffs

🏆 Playoff Format

Top seed advances directly to the Finals. Bottom seed is eliminated. Seeds 2–4 play a three-way playoff.

Three-Way Playoff: Seeds #2, #3, #4 compete — the team that wins the most matches advances to the Finals.

Tiebreakers: Regular season — highest winning percentage. Playoffs — total winning score across matches.

#5 seed does not qualify for the playoffs.
